About Voktis

Voktis is a UK-based IT services company providing reliable technology solutions for modern businesses. It specialises in managed IT support, cybersecurity, cloud services and Microsoft upgrades. Originally known as Techienology Ltd, the company rebranded to Voktis Ltd in November 2022 to reflect its growth. Voktis focuses on delivering secure, efficient and scalable IT systems tailored to each client’s needs. With a customer-centric approach, the company helps organisations strengthen their IT infrastructure and operate with confidence.It specialises in managed IT support, cybersecurity services, cloud infrastructure, Microsoft upgrades and a wide range of operational IT solutions that help businesses remain secure, productive and efficient.

Originally known as Techienology Ltd, the company underwent a strategic rebrand to Voktis Ltd in November 2022. This transition reflected its expansion, its evolving service portfolio and its long-term vision to serve clients with a stronger and more unified identity. The rebrand also marked an important milestone in the company’s journey toward becoming a trusted technology partner across multiple industries.

With a customer-centric approach at the core of its operations, Voktis works closely with businesses to strengthen their IT infrastructure, enhance digital resilience and build long-term technical confidence. Its goal is to ensure that every organisation it supports can operate smoothly, minimise downtime and unlock the full potential of modern technology
